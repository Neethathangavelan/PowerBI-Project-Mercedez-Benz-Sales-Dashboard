Project Overview

This Power BI project visualizes and analyzes Mercedes-Benz sales leads from multiple sources, including Website, Social Media, Walk-in, Event, and Referral. The dataset includes agent details, lead information, car models, budgets, follow-up activities, and lead status.

Dataset

The dataset contains the following columns:

Agent ID & Name: Sales agent handling the lead.

Lead ID & Name: Unique identifier and name of the lead.

Car Model: Vehicle of interest (A-Class, C-Class, E-Class, GLC, GLS, GLE, S-Class, EQC).

Lead Source: Origin of lead (Website, Referral, Social Media, Walk-in, Event).

Contact Information: Phone number and email of the lead.

Country: Lead location.

Lead Status: Current status (New, Contacted, Interested, Closed).

Budget $: Expected budget of the lead.

Follow-up Date: Planned follow-up date.

Calls & Emails: Number of interactions.

Car Model Image URL: Reference image for visualization.

Key Metrics & Visuals

Lead Status Analysis: Shows distribution of New, Contacted, Interested, and Closed leads.

Lead Source Insights: Highlights performance of different lead sources.

Budget Overview: Average and total budgets by car model and agent.

Agent Performance: Leads handled, conversion rate, and follow-up activity per agent.

Geographic Analysis: Leads by country with status mapping.

Car Model Popularity: Counts and budget insights for each car model.

Features

Interactive filters for Agent Name, Lead Status, Car Model, Lead Source, and Country.

Drill-through to individual lead details including contact info and follow-up history.

Visual comparison of calls and emails per lead for tracking engagement.

Image display for each car model in dashboard tiles.

Purpose

This dashboard helps the Mercedes-Benz sales team monitor lead progression, evaluate agent performance, identify high-potential leads, and optimize follow-ups to increase conversions.

Tools & Technologies

Power BI Desktop for dashboard development.

DAX for measures, calculations, and KPIs.

Data Visualization including bar charts, pie charts, maps, and tables.
